User research — interview session

Moderated usability session with U2, a newly qualified teacher (NQT). The task was to find Year 1 phonics reading comprehension resources starting from the homepage. The clip captures first contact with the existing navigation — showing the hover delay, the dual-function confusion on nav items, and the moment the user clicks through the mega menu rather than waiting for it.

3 / 3 participants

clicked the nav item directly rather than waiting for the hover-triggered mega menu to open.

47s mean task time

across 3 participants on an unguided navigation task. High variance (38s–56s) driven by the hover delay and menu confusion.

2 / 3 participants

commented unprompted that they expected clicking a nav item to navigate — not to trigger a dropdown.
